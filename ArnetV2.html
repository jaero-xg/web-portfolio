ArnetV2 (UI/UX)

ARnet is an augmented reality (AR) e-learning app developed for the Data Communication and Networking course. It uses marker-based AR technology to transform abstract networking concepts into interactive 3D visualizations, helping students better understand complex topics.

By scanning printed target images, users can view and interact with 3D models of networking components and processes in real space. This approach enhances engagement and improves comprehension, making technical concepts easier to grasp.

The project aims to bridge traditional learning with immersive technology, turning lessons into hands-on digital experiences that make studying more interactive and enjoyable.
